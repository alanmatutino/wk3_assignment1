Saipan's Must Try Restaurants

Food is something you always must experience when you travel, so in this article, I would like to give you my top picks on where to dine when you are visiting Saipan.

Best Places to Dine:

  1. Zoom Cafe
  2. Shirley's Coffee Shop
  3. Mango Six
  4. Etc
  5. Etc

Zoom Cafe | Susupe, Saipan

Zoom Cafe serves great fried chicken which is my personal favorite. They also serve a number of drinks and other types of deserts. They also have karaoke rooms that you and your friends could enjoy.

What I love:

  • Their chicken wings
  • Not too expensive
  • Their Karaoke room
